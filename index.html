About me

Xin chào, I'm Javier, a software developer born in Barcelona and based on planet earth. I’m hard-working, analytical and very passionate about software development. I was previously a networking specialist for over 15 years but my passion for software development made me choose a different path.
I decided to strengthen my skills and joined Microverse, where I acquired professional-level skills including Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React, Redux, Bootstrap and Test-Driven development techniques. During that time, I designed and developed more than 50 professional projects through pair programming with different coding partners remotely from around the world, and completed +70 coding challenges. I also mentored and assisted other students on their projects which gave me more confidence as a professional full-stack developer.
Being a chess lover helps me to prevent possible future problems and to think carefully about each of my next moves. I am also a science fiction book passionate, which makes me a person who is always willing to learn and evolve. Love to do sport and all kind of outdoor activities, but also like to give back something to the community helping whenever I can, especially if it is related to animal care. Travelling and meet people around the world is another of my passion, that makes me a globally minded person who thrives in diverse teams.
